Safe Hai

Whether you’re an individual or a business, our policies are designed to ensure that sending and receiving payments is simple and more secure. Now shop or sell beyond borders with confidence.
We take our customers’ security very seriously at PayPal.

Sell more safely.

  • Don’t include personal information when describing items for sale.
  • Make sure the funds are in your PayPal account before shipping the item.
  • Consider setting up a separate email address for sales and customer service so your personal email account remains private.
  • Make sure no personal details can be seen in the background of photographs of items for sale, e.g., house number or car number plates.
Protecting buyers

If an eligible item you’ve purchased online, does not arrive or doesn’t match the seller’s description, we’ll reimburse you for the full amount of the item plus shipping costs, as long as the eligibility requirements are met.

Protecting sellers

PayPal's Seller Protection helps protect eligible transactions against chargebacks, reversals due to fraud, and claims that items were not received raised through PayPal's Buyer Protection.

Our security advantages.

  • Detecting fraudulent transactions instantly, in real-time
  • Progressive system upgrades that keep us ahead of fraudsters
  • Extensive data encryption for strict confidentiality and security
  • Monitoring of transactions 24/7
  • Dedicated 2000-strong anti-fraud team of specialists globally
